# Product Overview — Video Analyzer

## What It Is
A web-based video analysis tool that lets users upload or link to videos and extract meaningful intelligence from them — transcripts, scene summaries, object detection highlights, sentiment analysis, key moment tagging, and exportable reports.

## Users
- Content creators and marketers analyzing video performance and content
- Researchers and journalists reviewing recorded footage
- Educators and students extracting insights from lecture or documentary videos
- Product teams reviewing user-research recordings and demos

## Positioning
The simplest way to turn any video into structured, searchable intelligence — no ML expertise required.

## Brand & Tone
- **Name:** Video Analyzer
- **Tone:** Clear, practical, confident — smart tool for smart people, without jargon
- **Personality:** Efficient and insightful; surfaces the signal, hides the noise

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Video Input** — Upload a local video file or paste a URL (YouTube, Vimeo, direct links)
2. **Auto Transcript** — Full speech-to-text transcript with timestamps
3. **Scene Summaries** — AI-generated summary per scene / chapter
4. **Key Moments** — Automatically tagged highlights (e.g., action, emotion, topic shifts)
5. **Sentiment Timeline** — Visual sentiment arc across the video duration
6. **Export** — Download transcript + summary as PDF or Markdown

## Scope & Constraints
- Single-page web app (HTML/CSS/JS); no backend required for MVP demo
- Simulated AI analysis for demo purposes (deterministic mock data driven by video metadata)
- Responsive design; works on desktop and tablet
- No user authentication in MVP

## Strategic Principles
1. Results first — the analysis output is the hero of every screen
2. One video at a time — focused, distraction-free workflow
3. Progressive disclosure — show the summary first, let users drill into details
4. Export always visible — the output must be portable
